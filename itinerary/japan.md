### IC Card / Tarjeta IC
- Get **Suica** or **PASMO** at Narita airport machines
- Works on: trains, buses, konbinis, vending machines, coin lockers
- *Funciona en trenes, buses, konbinis, máquinas expendedoras y lockers*
- Lyam (4y) and Diego (1y) ride **FREE** on all trains / *Viajan GRATIS en todos los trenes*
