## Luggage / Equipaje

### ZIPAIR Luggage Rules (SFO ↔ NRT)
| Type | Allowance | Dimensions |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Carry-on (2 bags) | **7 kg free** | 40×25×55 cm + 35×25×45 cm |
| Checked bag | **$85 USD per piece** (¥11,000 from Narita) | Standard |

- ⚠️ ZIPAIR is a low-cost carrier — no free checked bags!
- Weigh bags before arriving at the airport
- *ZIPAIR es aerolínea de bajo costo — no incluye maleta facturada gratis*

### Copa Airlines Luggage
- Check current allowance on copa.com for your fare class
- Infant (Diego) may have reduced luggage allowance

### Packing Strategy / Estrategia de Equipaje
- Pack light — you'll be on trains and metros with all your luggage
- Consider shipping luggage between cities in Japan (Takkyubin service / *servicio de envío de maletas*)
- *Empacar ligero. Van a estar cargando maletas en trenes y metros.*

## Visas for Colombian Passport / Visas para Pasaporte Colombiano

### 🇯🇵 Japan Visa — Embassy in Bogota

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Cost | **FREE** for Colombians / GRATIS |
| Processing | 13 business days (passports retained) |
| Validity | 3 months to enter Japan |
| Stay | 15, 30, or 90 days (based on itinerary) |
| Appointment | Required — [embjpcol.rsvsys.jp](https://embjpcol.rsvsys.jp/) |
| Hours | Mon–Fri 9:00–11:30 AM / 1:30–2:30 PM (submit) · 3:00–5:00 PM (pickup) |
| Embassy | Carrera 7 No. 71-21, Torre B Piso 11, Bogota |
| Phone | +57 (601) 743-3360 ext. 135 |
| Email | visa@ba.mofa.go.jp |

**Documents required / Documentos requeridos:**
- Passport (original — retained for 13 business days)
- Application form (hand-signed by each applicant; parents sign for kids)
- Recent photo
- Flight reservation (screenshot is fine, no need to purchase)
- Hotel reservation
- Bank statement / *Extracto bancario*

**Timeline / Fechas clave:**

| Milestone | Date |
|-----------|------|
| Earliest to apply | **June 3, 2026** (3 months before entry) |
| Latest to submit | **~August 12, 2026** (13 biz days before Sep 1 departure) |
| Recommended | **June 2026** — high demand, appointments fill fast |

> *⚠️ La visa de Japón es GRATIS pero toma 13 días hábiles y retienen el pasaporte. Tramitar en junio para evitar colas. Las citas se agotan rápido.*

### 🇨🇳 China Visa — Embassy in Bogota

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Cost | **~$311,000 COP per person** (reduced rate until Dec 2026) |
| Processing | 6 business days (normal) |
| Validity | 3 months (single entry) / 6 months (double entry) |
| Stay | 30 days per entry |
| Form | [cova.cs.mfa.gov.cn](https://cova.cs.mfa.gov.cn) — fill in Spanish, no corrections |
| Hours | Mon, Wed, Fri: 9:00–11:30 AM |
| Phone | +57 (601) 773-2500 (Mon–Fri 2–4 PM, Tue–Thu 9 AM–12 PM) |
| Email | bogota@csm.mfa.gov.cn |
| Embassy | Calle 72 #2A-41, Bogota |

**Documents required / Documentos requeridos:**
- E-passport (valid 6+ months, min 3 blank pages) + photocopy
- Application form (filled at cova.cs.mfa.gov.cn, in Spanish, no corrections)
- Color photo 3.5 × 4.5 cm, white background, glued to form
- Flight reservation or itinerary
- Hotel reservation
- Employment letter (position, salary, tenure) / *Carta laboral*
- Bank statement (last month) / *Extracto bancario del último mes*
- Chamber of Commerce certificate or bank certification
- **Yellow fever vaccination certificate** (mandatory — Colombia is endemic zone)

> *⚠️ La vacuna de fiebre amarilla es OBLIGATORIA para la visa china. Aplicar mínimo 6 días antes del trámite.*

**Timeline / Fechas clave:**

| Milestone | Date |
|-----------|------|
| Recommended start | **July 2026** |
| Latest to submit | **~August 6, 2026** (40 days before ~Sep 15 China entry) |
| Total cost (4 pax) | **~$1,244,000 COP** (~$311,000 × 4) |

## Packing for Kids / Empacar para los Niños

### Lyam (4 years old)
- Tablet + headphones + downloaded content for flights
- Favorite small toy for comfort
- Snacks he likes (for planes and transit)
- Light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes (2 pairs)
- Sunscreen + hat

### Diego (1 year old)
- Diapers (enough for first 2-3 days, buy locally after)
- Formula / baby food (if needed, available in both countries)
- Portable baby carrier (for temples, Great Wall, etc.)
- Light blanket for flights
- Baby medicine kit: fever reducer, oral rehydration, diaper cream
- Stroller (compact/travel type recommended)
- *Llevar cargador portátil para bebé — útil en la Muralla China y templos*

### Everyone / Para Todos
- Universal power adapter (Japan: Type A, China: Type A/I/C)
- Portable chargers / Power banks
- Reusable water bottles
- Small first-aid kit
- Motion sickness medicine (for Li River cruise, flights)
- Copies of all documents (physical + cloud storage)

## Tech Essentials / Tecnología Esencial

| Item | Japan | China |
|------|-------|-------|
| VPN | Not needed | **Critical** — download before |
| Google Maps | Works great | **Blocked** — use Amap (高德) |
| WhatsApp | Works | **Blocked** — use WeChat |
| Payments | Cash + credit card | **WeChat Pay / Alipay** |
| SIM/eSIM | Pocket WiFi or travel eSIM | Local SIM or eSIM |

### Recommended
- Get a **travel eSIM** that covers both Japan and China (e.g., Airalo, Nomad)
- Alternatively: pocket WiFi rental at Narita airport for Japan
- Download offline maps for both countries
- *Considerar eSIM de viaje que cubra ambos países*
